1. 30-Minute HIIT Workout

Complete 3 rounds of the following exercises:

  • Jumping Jacks: **30 seconds**
  • Burpees: **15 reps**
  • Mountain Climbers: **30 seconds**
  • Squats: **15 reps**

2. Full Body Strength Workout

Perform 3 sets of the following exercises:

  • Push-Ups: **10-15 reps**
  • Deadlifts: **10 reps** (Use dumbbells or a barbell)
  • Bench Press: **10 reps**
  • Lunges: **12 reps each leg**

3. Core Workout

Complete 3 rounds of the following exercises:

  • Plank: **30-60 seconds**
  • Russian Twists: **15 reps each side**
  • Leg Raises: **12 reps**
  • Bicycle Crunches: **15 reps each side**
